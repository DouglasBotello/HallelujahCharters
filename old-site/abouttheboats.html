About the Boat
25' Lookout


Captain Martin can take up to six passengers in this spacious vessel, though 1-5 anglers are preferred. Equipped with a lookout tower, she provides optimum views for scouting all sorts of varieties of fish.



According to Coast Guard law, there can be a total of seven people on this vessel; six passengers and Captain Martin. No acceptions apply.
